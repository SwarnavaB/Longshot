Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Longshot Finance ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, website at longshot.finance, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy. This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms & Conditions.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Full legal name, email address, phone number, date of birth, residential address, username and password
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued identification documents (passport, driver's license, national ID), photographs for identity verification, proof of address documents, tax identification numbers (where required)
  • Financial Information: Wallet addresses, transaction history on the Platform, trading preferences and settings
  • Communications: Support inquiries and correspondence, feedback and survey responses

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, browser type and version, app version
  • Usage Data: Pages visited and features used, time spent on the Platform, clickstream data
  • Transaction Data: Orders placed and executed, trading activity and patterns, wallet interactions, on-chain transaction data (publicly visible on the blockchain)

Information from Third Parties

  • Identity verification results and risk scores from KYC providers
  • Watchlist and sanctions screening results
  • Public blockchain transaction data
  • Aggregated analytics from service providers

2. How We Use Your Information

Provide and Improve Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process transactions and orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Personalize your experience
  • Operate and improve the Platform
  • Power AI-driven features including the AI Copilot

Security and Compliance

  • Verify your identity (KYC/AML compliance)
  • Prevent fraud, money laundering, and illegal activities
  • Monitor for suspicious activity
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Respond to legal requests and enforce our terms

Communications

  • Send transaction confirmations and alerts
  • Provide important updates about your account
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format

12. Blockchain and Decentralization

Public Nature of Blockchain

Transactions conducted through the Platform are recorded on public blockchains. This means:

  • Wallet addresses and transaction details are publicly visible
  • Transaction history is permanent and immutable
  • We cannot delete or modify on-chain data

Non-Custodial Architecture

As a non-custodial platform:

  • We do not have access to your private keys
  • We cannot recover lost or stolen assets
  • You are responsible for securing your wallet
